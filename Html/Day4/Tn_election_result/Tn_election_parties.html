Major Political Parties and Alliances in Tamil Nadu Election 2026

TVK – Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam

Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam is a newly formed political party led by actor Vijay. The party gained huge public attention during the 2026 election and focused on youth development, education, and social welfare.

Dmk alliance

The DMK alliance consisted of DMK, Congress, IUML, and allied parties. This alliance mainly focused on welfare schemes and state development.

AIADMK Alliance

The AIADMK alliance included AIADMK, PMK, BJP, and supporting parties. The alliance focused on infrastructure growth, employment, and economic improvement.

INC – Indian National Congress

Indian National Congress is a national political party that participated in the election as part of the DMK alliance and supported various welfare and development plans.

PMK – Pattali Makkal Katchi

Pattali Makkal Katchi is a regional political party in Tamil Nadu that mainly focuses on education, employment, and rural community welfare.

BJP – Bharatiya Janata Party

Bharatiya Janata Party is a national political party that contested the Tamil Nadu election through alliances and promoted development, digital growth, and economic policies.

IUML – Indian Union Muslim League

Indian Union Muslim League is a political party that contested as part of the DMK alliance and focused on minority welfare and social equality.
