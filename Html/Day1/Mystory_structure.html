Cinderella Story

Introduction

Once upon a time, in a small village, there lived a kind girl named Cinderella. After her father died, she lived with her cruel stepmother and two lazy stepsisters.They treated Cinderella like a servant. She cleaned the house, cooked food, and washed clothes all day, while her stepsisters wore beautiful dresses and enjoyed life.

Royal Ball

One day, the king announced a grand ball at the palace. The prince wanted to choose a bride, and every young woman in the kingdom was invited. Cinderella was very excited and wished to go, but her stepmother laughed at her and said, “You are not fit to attend the royal ball.” Then they left for the palace without her.

The Fairy GodMother

Cinderella sat alone and cried. Suddenly, her fairy godmother appeared with a shining wand. She magically turned a pumpkin into a golden carriage, mice into horses, and Cinderella’s old dress into a beautiful gown. She also gave her a pair of glass slippers. But the fairy godmother warned, “The magic will end at midnight.”

Cinderella at the Palace

At the palace, everyone was amazed by Cinderella’s beauty. The prince danced only with her the whole night. They talked happily and enjoyed the ball together. But when the clock struck midnight, Cinderella remembered the warning and quickly ran away. In her hurry, one glass slipper fell on the palace stairs.The Search for Cinderella.The prince searched the entire kingdom for the girl whose foot fit the glass slipper. Many girls tried, but it did not fit anyone. Finally, he reached Cinderella’s house. Her stepsisters tried the slipper, but it was too small. Then Cinderella tried it, and it fit perfectly. She also showed the second slipper she had kept safely.

The Happy Ending

The prince recognized her immediately and happily married her. Cinderella forgave her stepfamily and lived happily ever after in the palace

Moral

Kindness and patience are always rewarded.
