Short Story:“The Boy Who Brought Back the Lights"

Once there was a poor boy named Arjun in a small village. He was not good at studies, and everyone in class laughed at him. Even teachers thought he would never become successful.

But Arjun had one special habit — whenever something broke in the house, he tried to fix it. Radios, fans, clocks… he opened them carefully and learned how they worked. One day, a big storm hit the village.

article Electricity went off for two days. People were struggling. Arjun spent the whole night trying to repair an old generator lying in a shop. Everyone thought he would fail.
